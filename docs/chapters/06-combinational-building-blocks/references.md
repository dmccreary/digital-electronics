# References: Combinational Building Blocks

1. [Multiplexer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multiplexer) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of multiplexer design, selection logic, and applications.

2. [Binary decoder](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Binary_decoder) - Wikipedia - Detailed explanation of decoder circuits and their use in address decoding.

3. [Adder (electronics)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adder_(electronics)) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive coverage of half adders, full adders, ripple-carry adders, and carry-lookahead adders.

4. Digital Design (6th Edition) - M. Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Pearson - Chapter 5 covers combinational logic modules including multiplexers, decoders, encoders, and adders.

5. [Multiplexers](https://www.allaboutcircuits.com/textbook/digital/chpt-9/multiplexers/) - All About Circuits - Detailed coverage of MUX design, cascading, and using multiplexers to implement Boolean functions.

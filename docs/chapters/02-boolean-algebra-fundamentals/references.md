# References: Boolean Algebra Fundamentals

1. [Boolean algebra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boolean_algebra) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of Boolean algebra fundamentals, axioms, and theorems.

2. [De Morgan's laws](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/De_Morgan%27s_laws) - Wikipedia - Mathematical foundations and historical context of De Morgan's theorems with applications in logic and set theory.

3. Digital Design (6th Edition) - M. Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Pearson - Chapter 2 provides rigorous coverage of Boolean algebra axioms, theorems, and proof techniques.

4. [Boolean Algebra](https://www.allaboutcircuits.com/textbook/digital/chpt-7/introduction-boolean-algebra/) - All About Circuits - Free online textbook covering Boolean algebra laws with circuit-oriented examples and De Morgan's theorem applications.

5. [MIT 6.004 Computation Structures](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-004-computation-structures-spring-2017/) - MIT OpenCourseWare - University-level course materials covering Boolean algebra foundations for digital system design.

# References: Combinational Logic Design

1. [Combinational logic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Combinational_logic) - Wikipedia - Overview of combinational circuit theory, canonical forms, and the distinction from sequential logic.

2. [Canonical normal form](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canonical_normal_form) - Wikipedia - Detailed explanation of sum-of-products and product-of-sums canonical forms.

3. Digital Design (6th Edition) - M. Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Pearson - Chapters 3-4 cover combinational circuit analysis, SOP/POS forms, and design methodology.

4. [Combinational Logic Circuits](https://www.allaboutcircuits.com/textbook/digital/chpt-9/combinational-logic-circuits/) - All About Circuits - Free textbook covering combinational design from specification to implementation.

5. [MIT 6.111 Introductory Digital Systems Laboratory](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-111-introductory-digital-systems-laboratory-spring-2006/) - MIT OpenCourseWare - Lab-focused course materials on combinational design techniques.

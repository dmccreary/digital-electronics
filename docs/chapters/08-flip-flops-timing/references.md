# References: Flip-Flops and Timing

1. [Flip-flop (electronics)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flip-flop_(electronics)) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive coverage of flip-flop types, edge-triggering, and timing characteristics.

2. [Setup time and hold time](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flip-flop_(electronics)#Timing_considerations) - Wikipedia - Explanation of critical timing parameters for reliable flip-flop operation.

3. Digital Design (6th Edition) - M. Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Pearson - Chapter 6 covers flip-flop types, timing parameters, and synchronous design principles.

4. [Lesson 12: Setup and Hold Time](https://nandland.com/lesson-12-setup-and-hold-time/) - Nandland - Clear explanation of timing constraints with visual diagrams and FPGA implications.

5. [Flip Flops: Understanding D Flip Flop Logic & Timing](https://digilent.com/reference/learn/fundamentals/digital-logic/flip-flops/start) - Digilent Reference - Practical tutorial on flip-flop timing with FPGA implementation examples.

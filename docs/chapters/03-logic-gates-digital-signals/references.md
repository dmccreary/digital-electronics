# References: Logic Gates and Digital Signals

1. [Logic gate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Logic_gate) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of logic gates, symbols, truth tables, and implementations.

2. [CMOS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CMOS) - Wikipedia - Detailed explanation of CMOS technology used in modern digital circuits.

3. [Functional completeness](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Functional_completeness) - Wikipedia - Mathematical foundations explaining why NAND and NOR gates alone can implement any Boolean function.

4. Digital Design (6th Edition) - M. Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Pearson - Chapter 3 covers logic gates, gate characteristics, and IC families.

5. [Logic Gates](https://www.allaboutcircuits.com/textbook/digital/chpt-3/introduction-to-logic-gates/) - All About Circuits - Comprehensive coverage of all gate types with truth tables, symbols, and timing characteristics.

# References: Registers, Counters, and Datapath

1. [Shift register](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shift_register) - Wikipedia - Detailed coverage of shift register types, ring counters, and Johnson counters with applications.

2. [Counter (digital)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Counter_(digital)) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of binary counters, ripple counters, and synchronous counters.

3. [Ring counter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ring_counter) - Wikipedia - Explanation of ring and Johnson counter configurations.

4. Digital Design (6th Edition) - M. Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Pearson - Chapter 8 covers registers, shift registers, counters, and datapath components.

5. [Shift Registers](https://www.electronics-tutorials.ws/sequential/seq_5.html) - Electronics Tutorials - Comprehensive tutorial on SISO, SIPO, PISO, PIPO configurations and universal shift registers.

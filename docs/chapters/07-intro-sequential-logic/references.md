# References: Introduction to Sequential Logic

1. [Sequential logic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sequential_logic) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of sequential circuits and the distinction from combinational logic.

2. [Flip-flop (electronics)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flip-flop_(electronics)) - Wikipedia - Detailed coverage of bistable circuits, latches, and edge-triggered flip-flops.

3. Digital Design (6th Edition) - M. Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Pearson - Chapter 6 introduces sequential circuits, latches, and the distinction between combinational and sequential logic.

4. [Sequential Logic Circuits](https://www.electronics-tutorials.ws/sequential/seq_1.html) - Electronics Tutorials - Introduction to sequential circuits with SR latch and D latch analysis.

5. [Latches in Digital Logic](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/latches-in-digital-logic/) - GeeksforGeeks - Tutorial covering SR, gated SR, and D latches with truth tables and timing diagrams.
